About Hotel Sifi

"Established in 2012, Sifi Hotel sits gracefully on Haifa's Mt. Carmel. We've been a beacon of luxury and refined hospitality for over a decade.

Our enduring commitment spans from offering gourmet dining experiences crafted by renowned chefs to providing state-of-the-art amenities, all dedicated to celebrating and elevating each guest's unique stay with us."
